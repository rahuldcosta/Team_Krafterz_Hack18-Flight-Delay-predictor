Search by time
If departure or arrival time is your highest priority, select this option. The system displays flights across a wide range of time options. However, you may not see prices until you select all your flights. Depending on your site's configuration, the system may display equal-priced or lower-priced flights after you make your selections.
Search by price
If price is your highest priority, select this option. The system displays the lowest-priced flights, so you may see fewer options.
If you switch between searching by time and price, the system performs the search again and you lose any previously selected flights.

What is a Webfare?

Webfares are options that are normally only available directly from an airlines website.
We have made these fares available to you in this tool so you can browse all flights without having to go to an external site.
Webfares may include restrictions that do not apply to non-Webfares, like not being able to cancel or modify reservations.
